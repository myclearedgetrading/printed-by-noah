About Noah

I'm a young maker who turned a 3D printer into a little business — and I'm having a blast doing it.

Hey! I'm Noah.

I started 3D printing because I wanted to build things that didn't exist yet — toys, tools, funny desk buddies, useful gadgets, you name it. My family was my first “customers,” and then friends started asking for prints too. That's how Noah's 3D Print Shop started!

Running a shop is a huge learning adventure: designing, testing, fixing mistakes, and making the next version even better. I love seeing someone get excited about something I made.

I'm the one behind the printer — designing, testing, and shipping every piece you order from Noah's 3D Print Shop.

My favorite: custom orders. If you have a creative idea — even a weird one — you're my kind of customer.
