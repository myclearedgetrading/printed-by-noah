How it works

Custom orders are my favorite because they’re like mini quests. Here’s the simple process:

1
Submit Tell me what you want (and add a picture if you have one).
2
Noah reviews I check size, details, and what’s possible with my printer.
3
Confirm & print I message you with a plan and price, then I start printing!
4
Ship Your creation gets packed carefully and sent to you.
Friendly note: Response times can vary because I’m a kid running this shop, plus I’ve got school and life stuff. But I DO read every message and I’m excited to make your idea!
